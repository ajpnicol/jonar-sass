Paragon: ERP that doesn't suck

Awesome user experience

Awesome user experience

A lot of people use an ERP solution for the majority of their day, and we think they shouldn’t have to work with software that looks like it still lives in the 1990s. So, we’re making it real pretty. We want the digital elements within the software to resemble their real world counterparts. Why can’t the on-screen invoices look like those we see on paper, for example? Our new product appeals to the user’s intuition which will make the system easier to use without much training.

Simple customizations

Simple customizations

To overcome the boundaries of code-invasive customizations, we’ve developed this thing we like to call the "Rules Engine". It allows us to set conditions for your system, or even to customize a workflow engine that will suit processes that relate uniquely to your business. So, if you want to ship additional goods only when the moon is full and the shipper is wearing a blue sweater while dancing the jig, you can!

Easy implementations

Easy implementations

Paragon will sit on a first-class Cloud platform, which yields a number of benefits over the traditional ERP setup. Chief among those benefits is that implementations will be far easier. This also means our customers will have unlimited storage, so it can scale up (or down) on demand, automatically, to meet their needs.

No upfront costs

No surprise upfront costs

Paragon operates on the Cloud, so there is no need to purchase hardware. That means you can use the computer you already have and you don’t need a new server either. The best part of our pricing model is that there are no upfront costs. You pay per month for your subscription, so you can terminate your contract whenever you like. And since we’ve worked hard at making the interface user-friendly, the costs associated with training will dramatically fall too.
