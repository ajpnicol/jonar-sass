Become a partner

Let’s be honest. Selling an ERP system is not an easy job. They’re incredibly expensive tools that promise to make the customer’s lives better. But they don’t. And even though it’s usually the system that sucks, as a seller and servicer of ERP, you often find yourself on the receiving end of endless complaints and an assembly of grumpy customers.


We know that ERP sucks. So we went back to the drawing board and reinvented it. Now we’re looking for awesome partners who can be proud to sell and service this awesome system we call Paragon.

So, why should you become a Paragon VAR?

Paragon is awesome

Paragon is much easier to sell than any traditional ERP system – it’s less expensive, easier on the eye, and frankly, just better. Paragon was designed to specifically address the things that people hate most about ERP systems. So, whether you’re a user or someone implementing it, Paragon will come as a wave of relief.

With Paragon in your toolbox, you’ll also gain access to a huge untapped market, because it’s specifically targeted at small and medium-sized businesses who are desperately seeking a system capable of managing their unique needs.

Stop jumping through hoops

We’re not a rules and regulations kind of company, so we don’t believe in having our partners jump through a million hoops, run a marathon, bend over backwards, and then change their entire business model to sell and service Paragon. We want to make it as easy as possible to get started and we believe in taking a collaborative approach when it comes to working with you.

Make lots of money

We’ve done away with the traditional sales commission compensation model. Paragon is a subscription-based application so, as a valued partner, we don’t see why you shouldn’t also benefit from a recurring revenue stream. At the same time, you’ll retain all of the revenue associated with servicing Paragon.
