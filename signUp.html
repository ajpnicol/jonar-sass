Beta FAQs

What is a Beta customer?

A Beta customer participates in an important phase of software development - the awkward adolescence stage a software product faces before it is finalized and fully launched.

What type of companies can become Beta customers

We’re looking for forward thinking companies who are willing to invest the time and effort to collaborate with us in making Paragon flawless for its launch. While our Beta users are suckers for new technology, they are also incredibly patient while we work out the bugs in the system.

What do I need to do as a Beta user?

Basically, you try to break the software. We’ll want you to tell us what you like and don’t like, what it’s missing, and how it compares with your expectations.

If I become a Beta user, what happens afterwards?

Once the beta period is over, you become one of our amazing customers. We’ll forever be grateful of your feedback, and we’ll make sure you know it.

What’s in it for me?

You’ll have input into the features to ensure Paragon meets your unique needs. Plus, you’ll get a discount when Paragon is ready to launch.

Channel Partner FAQs

What is a channel partner?

Our channel partners are value-added resellers of our software system, Paragon. Our partners introduce Paragon to existing or potential new customers. If they like it, our partners are their first point of contact throughout the implementation process, and for the duration of the customer’s engagement with Paragon.

What is expected of channel partners?

Our partners are the front line. They’re the ones implementing Paragon. They’re the ones interacting with Paragon users on a daily basis. They’re the ones relieving users of their everyday pains. They’re the ones we’re proud to partner with to provide incredible services to Paragon users.

Who is eligible to be a partner?

We don’t make long and unnecessary checklists. If you’re focused on your customers and dedicated to giving them excellent quality services, you’re already in the running. There’s not really much else to it.

What’s in it for me?

All our partners benefit from a mutually beneficial arrangement with a forward-thinking ERP software developer. You’ll be able to introduce an awesome product with great value to all your existing and prospective customers. You’ll get all the service fees from these customers, and you’ll earn a commission on the user fees too.

How will our partnership work?

As cliché as it may sound, your success is our success. That means we’ll be there to support you whenever you need it. We’ll help you with escalated cases, provide you with an abundance of sales tools and support, and we’ll ensure you’re equipped to meet the needs of your customers with training and documentation.
